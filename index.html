Our Story

Dream Line Global Consultancy

Founded by Dr. Hussain, Dream Line Global Consultancy was established with a singular vision — to create a bridge between exceptional talent in India and elite employment opportunities across the UAE and GCC region.

With offices in Abu Dhabi (Mazyad Mall, Tower 2, Office 626) and Srinagar (Khanyar), we operate at the intersection of two dynamic markets — bringing precision, integrity and results to every placement.
